## 2. Human evaluation

To evaluate the consistency of the extracted grammar types with human intuition, we sampled 200 instances from the test set of NLPCC 2018, and then asked two Chinese linguistic experts to manually assess the automatically extracted edits and their assigned error types. The assessment scores range from 0 (bad), 1 (acceptable) to 2 (good). We obtain the human evaluation as shown in the following table. The averaged evaluation scores of these two raters are 1.69 and 1.88, respectively.
